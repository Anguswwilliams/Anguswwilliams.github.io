Group Game Project

For our final project we had 3 weeks to split into teams of 3-4 and design a video game that runs in the browser using entirely HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I teamed up with classmates Jock, Ethan, and Colin to design it. In just one week we had our MVP and by week three we were finished. We decided to name it "mahnamejoks" after our graphic designer, Jock. The aim of the game is to survive all 25 waves of monsters, each wave harder than the last. You start out with nothing but a slingshot but get power-ups at the end of every wave. Music for the game was provided by the DE music appreciation class.
