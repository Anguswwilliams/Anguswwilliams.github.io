Delicious Mac'n Cheese Recipe

Materials

  1. Pan
  2. Spatula
  3. Stovetop
  4. Skillet
  5. Measuring cup
  6. Cooking spray

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
1 1/2 Cups All purpose flour
3 1/2 tsp Baking powder
1 tsp Salt
1 tbl White sugar
1 1/4 cups Milk
1 Egg
3 tbl Butter

Instructions

To make the pancakes, first whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar in a large bowl. In another bowl, mix the milk, egg, and melted butter. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and gently fold until just combined; don't overmix. Heat an oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat and pour in 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake, cooking for 2-3 minutes per side and flipping when bubbles appear.
