Delicious Mac'n Cheese Recipe

Materials

  1. Saucepan
  2. Pot
  3. Whisk
  4. Colander
  5. Measuring cups/spoons

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
2 cups Elbow pasta
Shredded Cheddar
2 tbl Butter
2 tbl All purspose flour
2 cups Milk
1/2 tsp Salt
1/4 tsp Black pepper

Instructions

Boil a pot of water, add a pinch of salt and 2 cups of elbow macaroni, and cook until the pasta is soft, then drain and set aside. In a saucepan, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat, whisk in 2 tablespoons of flour until smooth, then add 2 cups of milk, while whisking to remove clumps. Cook until thickened, then add 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper. Lower the heat and add 2 cups of shredded cheese until its melted and smooth. Combine the pasta with the cheese sauce, and stir.
