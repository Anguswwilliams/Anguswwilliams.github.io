## What genre will your game be? 

Action/fighting, Survival.

## What do you want your music to sound like? Write four words or phrases that capture the vibe you're going for. 

Action, Surviving against enemies, Fast Paced, Tense.

## What are the game objectives? Will there be a score? How do you win or lose?

There will be a score based on the amount of rooms you pass, you win by getting to room 100. 

## How will the player interact with the game?

The player will use the WASD keys to move through rooms and IJKL or arrow keys to shoot their slingshot.

## What features will you include in your MVP?

Base combat and most likely first 25 rooms.
